# 2. Energy Scaling

## a) Infinite Potential Well

The energy levels are

$$
E_n=\frac{n^2h^2}{8mL^2}
$$

Therefore

$$
E_n\propto n^2
$$

### Answer

$$
\boxed{E_n\propto n^2}
$$

---

## b) Hydrogen Atom

The energy levels are

$$
E_n=-\frac{13.6}{n^2}\ \text{eV}
$$

Therefore

$$
E_n\propto\frac{1}{n^2}
$$

### Answer

$$
\boxed{E_n\propto n^{-2}}
$$
