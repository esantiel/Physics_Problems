# 4. Radioactive Half-Life

Half-life:

$$
T_{1/2}=5.27\ \text{years}
$$

Initial mass:

$$
m_0=100\ \text{g}
$$

Time elapsed:

$$
t=21\ \text{years}
$$

Number of half-lives:

$$
n=\frac{21}{5.27}
\approx3.98
$$

Remaining mass:

$$
m=m_0\left(\frac12\right)^n
$$

$$
m=100\left(\frac12\right)^{3.98}
$$

$$
m\approx6.3\ \text{g}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{m\approx6.3\ \text{g}}
$$
