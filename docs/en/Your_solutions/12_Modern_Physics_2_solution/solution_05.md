# 5. Alpha Decay

An alpha particle is

$$
{}^{4}_{2}\text{He}
$$

For Uranium-238:

$$
{}^{238}_{92}\text{U}
\rightarrow
{}^{234}_{90}\text{Th}
+
{}^{4}_{2}\text{He}
$$

Check conservation:

Mass number:

$$
238=234+4
$$

Atomic number:

$$
92=90+2
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{
{}^{238}_{92}\text{U}
\rightarrow
{}^{234}_{90}\text{Th}
+
{}^{4}_{2}\text{He}
}
$$
