# 3. Photon Energy

Given:

$$
\lambda=500\ \text{nm}
$$

Convert to meters:

$$
\lambda=5.00\times10^{-7}\ \text{m}
$$

Using

$$
E=\frac{hc}{\lambda}
$$

Substitute:

$$
E=
\frac{
(4.1357\times10^{-15})
(3.0\times10^8)
}
{5.00\times10^{-7}}
$$

$$
E\approx2.48\ \text{eV}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{E\approx2.48\ \text{eV}}
$$
