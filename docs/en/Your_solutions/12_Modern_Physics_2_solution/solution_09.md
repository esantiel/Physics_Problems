# 9. Quantum Well Energy

Width:

$$
L=0.5\ \text{nm}
=5.0\times10^{-10}\ \text{m}
$$

State:

$$
n=2
$$

Energy formula:

$$
E_n=
\frac{n^2h^2}
{8mL^2}
$$

Substituting:

$$
E_2=
\frac{
4(6.626\times10^{-34})^2
}
{
8(9.11\times10^{-31})
(5.0\times10^{-10})^2
}
$$

$$
E_2
\approx9.64\times10^{-19}\ \text{J}
$$

Convert to eV:

$$
E_2=
\frac{9.64\times10^{-19}}
{1.602\times10^{-19}}
$$

$$
E_2\approx6.02\ \text{eV}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{E_2\approx6.0\ \text{eV}}
$$
