# 7. Pair Annihilation

Rest energy of each particle:

$$
0.511\ \text{MeV}
$$

Total energy:

$$
E_{tot}=2(0.511)
$$

$$
E_{tot}=1.022\ \text{MeV}
$$

Two identical photons are produced:

$$
E_\gamma=\frac{1.022}{2}
$$

$$
E_\gamma=0.511\ \text{MeV}
$$

Convert to eV:

$$
E_\gamma=5.11\times10^5\ \text{eV}
$$

Wavelength:

$$
\lambda=\frac{1240}{5.11\times10^5}
$$

$$
\lambda=2.43\times10^{-3}\ \text{nm}
$$

## Final Answer

Photon energy:

$$
\boxed{0.511\ \text{MeV}}
$$

Photon wavelength:

$$
\boxed{2.43\times10^{-3}\ \text{nm}}
$$
