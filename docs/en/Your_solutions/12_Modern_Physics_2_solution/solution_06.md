# 6. Beta Decay

In beta-minus decay:

$$
n\rightarrow p+e^-+\bar{\nu}_e
$$

For Carbon-14:

$$
{}^{14}_{6}\text{C}
\rightarrow
{}^{14}_{7}\text{N}
+
e^-
+
\bar{\nu}_e
$$

Check conservation:

Mass number:

$$
14=14
$$

Charge:

$$
6=7-1
$$
