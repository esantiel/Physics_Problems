# 7. Velocity Transformation

Rocket speed:

$$
u=0.8c
$$

Probe speed relative to rocket:

$$
v'=0.5c
$$

## Classical Mechanics

$$
v=u+v'
$$

$$
v=0.8c+0.5c
$$

$$
v=1.3c
$$

---

## Special Relativity

Velocity addition formula:

$$
v=
\frac{u+v'}
{1+\frac{uv'}{c^2}}
$$

Substituting:

$$
v=
\frac{0.8c+0.5c}
{1+(0.8)(0.5)}
$$

$$
v=
\frac{1.3c}{1.4}
$$

$$
v\approx0.929c
$$

## Final Answer

Classical:

$$
\boxed{1.3c}
$$

Relativistic:

$$
\boxed{0.929c}
$$
