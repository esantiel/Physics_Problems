# 1. Length Contraction

Proper length:

$$
L_0 = 1.0\ \text{m}
$$

Observed length:

$$
L = 0.5\ \text{m}
$$

Length contraction formula:

$$
L = L_0\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}
$$

Substituting:

$$
0.5 = 1.0\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}
$$

Squaring both sides:

$$
0.25 = 1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}
$$

$$
\frac{v^2}{c^2}=0.75
$$

$$
v=c\sqrt{0.75}
$$

$$
v\approx0.866c
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{v \approx 0.866c}
$$
