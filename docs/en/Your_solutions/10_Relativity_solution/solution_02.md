# 2. Time Dilation

The Earth device plays 2 hours while the spaceship device plays 1 hour.

Thus:

$$
\gamma = \frac{t}{t_0}
= \frac{2}{1}
= 2
$$

Lorentz factor:

$$
\gamma=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}}
$$

Substitute:

$$
2=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}}
$$

$$
\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}=\frac12
$$

$$
1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}=\frac14
$$

$$
\frac{v^2}{c^2}=\frac34
$$

$$
v=\sqrt{\frac34}c
$$

$$
v\approx0.866c
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{v\approx0.866c}
$$
