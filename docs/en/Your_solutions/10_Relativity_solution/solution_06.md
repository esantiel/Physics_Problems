# 6. Mass-Energy

Mass converted:

$$
m=0.7\ \text{g}
=7\times10^{-4}\ \text{kg}
$$

Einstein's equation:

$$
E=mc^2
$$

$$
E=(7\times10^{-4})(3\times10^8)^2
$$

$$
E=6.3\times10^{13}\ \text{J}
$$

Convert to kilotons TNT:

$$
1\ \text{kt}
=
4.184\times10^{12}\ \text{J}
$$

$$
\frac{6.3\times10^{13}}
{4.184\times10^{12}}
\approx15.1
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{E\approx6.3\times10^{13}\ \text{J}}
$$

$$
\boxed{\approx15\ \text{kilotons TNT}}
$$
