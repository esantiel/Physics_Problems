# 5. Relativistic Mass

Rest mass:

$$
m_0=50\ \text{kg}
$$

Speed:

$$
v=0.999c
$$

Lorentz factor:

$$
\gamma
=
\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-0.999^2}}
$$

$$
\gamma\approx22.37
$$

Relativistic mass:

$$
m=\gamma m_0
$$

$$
m=(22.37)(50)
$$

$$
m\approx1118\ \text{kg}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{m\approx1.12\times10^3\ \text{kg}}
$$
