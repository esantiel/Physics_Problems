# 3. Time Dilation in Real Life

Muon lifetime in its rest frame:

$$
\tau_0=2.20\ \mu s
$$

Speed:

$$
v=0.990c
$$

Lorentz factor:

$$
\gamma
=
\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-0.99^2}}
$$

$$
\gamma\approx7.09
$$

Dilated lifetime:

$$
\tau=\gamma\tau_0
$$

$$
\tau=(7.09)(2.20)
$$

$$
\tau\approx15.6\ \mu s
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{\tau\approx15.6\ \mu s}
$$
