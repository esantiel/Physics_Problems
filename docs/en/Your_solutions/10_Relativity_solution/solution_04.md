# 4. High-Energy Physics

Proton rest energy:

$$
E_0=938\ \text{MeV}
$$

Speed:

$$
v=0.99c
$$

Lorentz factor:

$$
\gamma
=
\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-0.99^2}}
\approx7.09
$$

Kinetic energy:

$$
K=(\gamma-1)mc^2
$$

$$
K=(7.09-1)(938)
$$

$$
K\approx5710\ \text{MeV}
$$

Convert to GeV:

$$
K\approx5.71\ \text{GeV}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{K\approx5.7\ \text{GeV}}
$$
