# 10. Speed of Light in Media

The index of refraction of diamond is:

$$
n = 2.42
$$

We need to find the speed of light in diamond.

---

## Formula

The speed of light in a medium is:

$$
v = \frac{c}{n}
$$

where:

- $v$ is the speed of light in the medium
- $c = 3.0 \times 10^8 \ \text{m/s}$ is the speed of light in vacuum
- $n$ is the index of refraction

---

## Substitute the Values

$$
v = \frac{3.0 \times 10^8}{2.42}
$$

$$
v \approx 1.24 \times 10^8 \ \text{m/s}
$$

---

# Final Answer

The speed of light in diamond is:

$$
\boxed{v \approx 1.24 \times 10^8 \ \text{m/s}}
$$

---

## Conclusion

Light travels slower in diamond than in vacuum because diamond has a high refractive index.
