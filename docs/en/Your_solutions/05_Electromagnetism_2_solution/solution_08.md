# 8. EM Spectrum

We need to list the electromagnetic radiation types in order of **increasing wavelength**.

Increasing wavelength means:

$$
\text{shortest wavelength} \rightarrow \text{longest wavelength}
$$

---

## Order of Electromagnetic Radiation

The order is:

$$
\boxed{
\text{Gamma rays}
\rightarrow
\text{X-rays}
\rightarrow
\text{Ultraviolet}
\rightarrow
\text{Infrared}
\rightarrow
\text{Microwaves}
\rightarrow
\text{Radio waves}
}
$$

---

## Final Answer

```text
Gamma rays → X-rays → Ultraviolet → Infrared → Microwaves → Radio waves
