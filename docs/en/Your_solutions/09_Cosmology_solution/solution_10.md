# 10. Doppler Effect

Given:

$$
v=5\times10^6\ \text{m/s}
$$

$$
c=3\times10^8\ \text{m/s}
$$

$$
\lambda_e=486\ \text{nm}
$$

---

## Non-Relativistic Formula

$$
\lambda_o
=
\lambda_e
\left(
1+\frac{v}{c}
\right)
$$

$$
\lambda_o
=
486
\left(
1+\frac{5\times10^6}{3\times10^8}
\right)
$$

$$
\lambda_o
=
494.1\ \text{nm}
$$

---

## Relativistic Formula

$$
\lambda_o
=
486
\sqrt{
\frac{1+\frac{v}{c}}
{1-\frac{v}{c}}
}
$$

$$
\lambda_o
\approx494.2\ \text{nm}
$$

---

## Final Answer

Non-relativistic:

$$
\boxed{494.1\ \text{nm}}
$$

Relativistic:

$$
\boxed{494.2\ \text{nm}}
$$

Difference:

$$
\boxed{0.1\ \text{nm}}
$$
