# 6. Galactic Collision

Distance:

$$
d=2.5\times10^6\ \text{ly}
$$

Convert to km:

$$
d\approx2.37\times10^{19}\ \text{km}
$$

Approach speed:

$$
v=110\ \text{km/s}
$$

Time:

$$
t=\frac{d}{v}
$$

$$
t\approx2.15\times10^{17}\ \text{s}
$$

Convert to years:

$$
t\approx6.8\times10^9\ \text{years}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{6.8\ \text{billion years}}
$$
