# 8. Galactic Trivia

## a) Diameter-to-Thickness Ratio

Milky Way:

$$
D\approx100000\ \text{ly}
$$

Thickness:

$$
h\approx1000\ \text{ly}
$$

Ratio:

$$
\frac{D}{h}
=
100
$$

### Answer

$$
\boxed{100:1}
$$

---

## b) Stars Per Person

Stars:

$$
2\times10^{11}
$$

Humans:

$$
8\times10^9
$$

Stars per person:

$$
\frac{2\times10^{11}}
{8\times10^9}
=
25
$$

### Answer

$$
\boxed{25}
$$

---

## c) Counting Stars

Count rate:

$$
1\ \text{star/s}
$$

Total time:

$$
2\times10^{11}\ \text{s}
$$

Convert to years:

$$
\frac{2\times10^{11}}
{3.156\times10^7}
\approx6340
$$

### Answer

$$
\boxed{6340\ \text{years}}
$$
