# 4. Hubble's Law

Using

$$
v = H_0 d
$$

Set

$$
v = c = 300000 \text{ km/s}
$$

and

$$
H_0 = 70 \text{ (km/s)/Mpc}
$$

Then

$$
d=\frac{c}{H_0}
$$

$$
d=\frac{300000}{70}
$$

$$
d\approx4286\ \text{Mpc}
$$

Converting:

$$
1\ \text{Mpc}=3.26\times10^6\ \text{ly}
$$

$$
d\approx1.40\times10^{10}\ \text{ly}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{4286\ \text{Mpc}}
$$

$$
\boxed{14\ \text{billion light-years}}
$$
