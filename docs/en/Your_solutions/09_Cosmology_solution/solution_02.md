# 2. Cosmic Temperatures

Approximate temperatures:

| Object | Temperature |
|----------|----------|
| Universe during nucleosynthesis | $10^9$ K |
| Core of the Sun | $1.5\times10^7$ K |
| Ionization temperature of hydrogen | $\sim 10^4$ K |
| Surface of the Sun | $5800$ K |
| Universe at recombination | $3000$ K |
| Human body | $310$ K |
| Present CMB | $2.7$ K |
| Interstellar space | $\sim 2$ K |
| 10-solar-mass black hole | $\sim 6\times10^{-9}$ K |
