# 5. Galactic Year

Distance from galactic center:

$$
R=27000\ \text{ly}
$$

Convert to km:

$$
R\approx2.55\times10^{17}\ \text{km}
$$

Orbital circumference:

$$
C=2\pi R
$$

$$
C\approx1.60\times10^{18}\ \text{km}
$$

Speed:

$$
v=220\ \text{km/s}
$$

Time:

$$
T=\frac{C}{v}
$$

$$
T\approx7.3\times10^{15}\ \text{s}
$$

Convert to years:

$$
T\approx2.3\times10^8\ \text{years}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{230\ \text{million years}}
$$
