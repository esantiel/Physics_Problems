# 13. Transformer Ratio

## Given

$$
V_p = 120\,\text{V}
$$

$$
V_s = 9.0\,\text{V}
$$

$$
N_p = 400
$$

For a transformer:

$$
\frac{V_s}{V_p} = \frac{N_s}{N_p}
$$

Solve for $N_s$:

$$
N_s = N_p \frac{V_s}{V_p}
$$

Substitute:

$$
N_s = 400 \frac{9.0}{120}
$$

$$
N_s = 30
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{N_s = 30 \text{ turns}}
$$
