# 10. Average Current

## Given

$$
Q = 30\,\text{C}
$$

$$
t = 2\,\text{ms} = 2 \times 10^{-3}\,\text{s}
$$

Average current is:

$$
I_{\text{avg}} = \frac{Q}{t}
$$

Substitute:

$$
I_{\text{avg}} = \frac{30}{2 \times 10^{-3}}
$$

$$
I_{\text{avg}} = 15000\,\text{A}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{I_{\text{avg}} = 1.5 \times 10^4\,\text{A}}
$$

or:

$$
\boxed{I_{\text{avg}} = 15000\,\text{A}}
$$
