# 11. Power and Energy

## Given

$$
R = 100\,\Omega
$$

$$
V = 50\,\text{V}
$$

$$
t = 5\,\text{min} = 300\,\text{s}
$$

---

## Power

$$
P = \frac{V^2}{R}
$$

$$
P = \frac{50^2}{100}
$$

$$
P = 25\,\text{W}
$$

---

## Energy

$$
E = Pt
$$

$$
E = (25)(300)
$$

$$
E = 7500\,\text{J}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{P = 25\,\text{W}}
$$

$$
\boxed{E = 7500\,\text{J}}
$$

or:

$$
\boxed{E = 7.5\,\text{kJ}}
$$
