# 9. Current

## Given

$$
Q(t) = 5t^2 + 5
$$

Current is the derivative of charge:

$$
I(t) = \frac{dQ}{dt}
$$

Differentiate:

$$
I(t) = \frac{d}{dt}(5t^2+5)
$$

$$
I(t) = 10t
$$

At:

$$
t = 3\,\text{s}
$$

$$
I(3) = 10(3)
$$

$$
I(3) = 30\,\text{A}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{I = 30\,\text{A}}
$$
