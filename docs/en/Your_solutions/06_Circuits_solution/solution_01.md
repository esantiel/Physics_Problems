# 1. Series and Parallel Circuit

## Given

$$
R_1 = 15\,\Omega
$$

$$
R_2 = 30\,\Omega
$$

$$
R_3 = 50\,\Omega
$$

$$
V = 12\,\text{V}
$$

---

## Series Connection

For resistors in series:

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = R_1 + R_2 + R_3
$$

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = 15 + 30 + 50
$$

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = 95\,\Omega
$$

Current from the battery:

$$
I = \frac{V}{R_{\text{eq}}}
$$

$$
I = \frac{12}{95}
$$

$$
I \approx 0.126\,\text{A}
$$

### Final Answer for Series

$$
\boxed{R_{\text{eq}} = 95\,\Omega}
$$

$$
\boxed{I \approx 0.126\,\text{A}}
$$

---

## Parallel Connection

For resistors in parallel:

$$
\frac{1}{R_{\text{eq}}}
=
\frac{1}{15}
+
\frac{1}{30}
+
\frac{1}{50}
$$

$$
\frac{1}{R_{\text{eq}}}
=
0.12
$$

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = \frac{1}{0.12}
$$

$$
R_{\text{eq}} \approx 8.33\,\Omega
$$

Current from the battery:

$$
I = \frac{V}{R_{\text{eq}}}
$$

$$
I = \frac{12}{8.33}
$$

$$
I \approx 1.44\,\text{A}
$$

### Final Answer for Parallel

$$
\boxed{R_{\text{eq}} \approx 8.33\,\Omega}
$$

$$
\boxed{I \approx 1.44\,\text{A}}
$$
