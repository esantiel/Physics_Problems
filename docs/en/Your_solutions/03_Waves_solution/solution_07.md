## 7. Standing Wave Modes

A string has length:

$$
L = 80 \text{ cm} = 0.80 \text{ m}
$$

The standing wave has **four antinodes**, so this is the **4th harmonic**:

$$
n = 4
$$

For a string fixed at both ends:

$$
L = n \frac{\lambda}{2}
$$

Substitute $n = 4$:

$$
0.80 = 4 \cdot \frac{\lambda}{2}
$$

$$
0.80 = 2\lambda
$$

$$
\lambda = \frac{0.80}{2}
$$

$$
\lambda = 0.40 \text{ m}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\lambda = 0.40 \text{ m}
$$
