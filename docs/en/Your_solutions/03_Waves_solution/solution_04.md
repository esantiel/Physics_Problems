## 4. Phase Difference

The phase difference between two points on a wave is given by:

$$
\Delta \phi = 2\pi \frac{\Delta x}{\lambda}
$$

Here, the separation is:

$$
\Delta x = \frac{\lambda}{3}
$$

Substitute into the formula:

$$
\Delta \phi = 2\pi \frac{\lambda/3}{\lambda}
$$

$$
\Delta \phi = 2\pi \cdot \frac{1}{3}
$$

$$
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{3}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{3} \text{ rad}
$$
