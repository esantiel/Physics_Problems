## 5. Echo Ranging

A person hears the echo:

$$
t = 1 \text{ s}
$$

The speed of sound in air is:

$$
v = 343 \text{ m/s}
$$

Since the sound travels to the cliff and back, the total distance is:

$$
d_{\text{total}} = vt
$$

$$
d_{\text{total}} = 343 \times 1
$$

$$
d_{\text{total}} = 343 \text{ m}
$$

The distance to the cliff is half of that:

$$
d = \frac{343}{2}
$$

$$
d = 171.5 \text{ m}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
d = 171.5 \text{ m}
$$
