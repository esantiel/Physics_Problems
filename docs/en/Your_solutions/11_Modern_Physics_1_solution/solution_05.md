# 5. Heisenberg Principle

Electron confined to:

$$
\Delta x=0.1\ \text{nm}
$$

$$
\Delta x=1.0\times10^{-10}\ \text{m}
$$

Heisenberg relation:

$$
\Delta x\Delta p\ge\frac{\hbar}{2}
$$

Minimum momentum uncertainty:

$$
\Delta p=
\frac{\hbar}{2\Delta x}
$$

$$
\Delta p=
\frac{1.055\times10^{-34}}
{2(1.0\times10^{-10})}
$$

$$
\Delta p=5.27\times10^{-25}
\ \text{kg m/s}
$$

Velocity uncertainty:

$$
\Delta v=\frac{\Delta p}{m_e}
$$

$$
\Delta v=
\frac{5.27\times10^{-25}}
{9.11\times10^{-31}}
$$

$$
\Delta v\approx5.8\times10^5\ \text{m/s}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{\Delta v\approx5.8\times10^5\ \text{m/s}}
$$
