# 6. Emission Spectra

Hydrogen energy levels:

$$
E_n=-\frac{13.6}{n^2}\ \text{eV}
$$

For

$$
n=4
$$

$$
E_4=-0.85\ \text{eV}
$$

For

$$
n=2
$$

$$
E_2=-3.40\ \text{eV}
$$

Photon energy:

$$
E_\gamma=E_4-E_2
$$

$$
E_\gamma=2.55\ \text{eV}
$$

Wavelength:

$$
\lambda=\frac{1240}{E}
$$

$$
\lambda=\frac{1240}{2.55}
$$

$$
\lambda\approx486\ \text{nm}
$$

This is the H-beta line.

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{E_\gamma=2.55\ \text{eV}}
$$

$$
