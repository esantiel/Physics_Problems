# 4. X-Ray Production

Required wavelength:

$$
\lambda=0.01\ \text{nm}
$$

$$
\lambda=1.0\times10^{-11}\ \text{m}
$$

Photon energy:

$$
E=\frac{hc}{\lambda}
$$

$$
E=
\frac{(6.626\times10^{-34})(3.00\times10^8)}
{1.0\times10^{-11}}
$$

$$
E=1.99\times10^{-14}\ \text{J}
$$

Since

$$
E=qV
$$

$$
V=\frac{E}{e}
$$

$$
V=
\frac{1.99\times10^{-14}}
{1.602\times10^{-19}}
$$

$$
V\approx1.24\times10^5\ \text{V}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{V\approx124\ \text{kV}}
$$
