# 10. Photoelectric Effect

Work function of potassium:

$$
\phi=2.3\ \text{eV}
$$

Wavelength:

$$
\lambda=350\ \text{nm}
$$

Photon energy:

$$
E=\frac{1240}{350}
$$

$$
E=3.54\ \text{eV}
$$

Maximum kinetic energy:

$$
K_{\max}=E-\phi
$$

$$
K_{\max}=3.54-2.3
$$

$$
K_{\max}=1.24\ \text{eV}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{K_{\max}=1.24\ \text{eV}}
$$
