# 2. Macroscopic Limits

Mass of golf ball:

$$
m=50\ \text{g}=0.050\ \text{kg}
$$

Speed:

$$
v=60\ \text{m/s}
$$

Momentum:

$$
p=mv
$$

$$
p=(0.050)(60)
$$

$$
p=3.0\ \text{kg m/s}
$$

De Broglie wavelength:

$$
\lambda=\frac{h}{p}
$$

$$
\lambda=\frac{6.626\times10^{-34}}{3.0}
$$

$$
\lambda\approx2.21\times10^{-34}\ \text{m}
$$

## Why Quantum Effects Are Not Visible

This wavelength is enormously smaller than atomic dimensions:

$$
10^{-34}\ \text{m} \ll 10^{-10}\ \text{m}
$$

Therefore diffraction and interference effects are completely negligible.

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{\lambda\approx2.2\times10^{-34}\ \text{m}}
$$
