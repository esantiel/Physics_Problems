# 8. Angular Momentum

Bohr quantization:

$$
L=n\hbar
$$

For

$$
n=4
$$

$$
L=4\hbar
$$

Using

$$
\hbar=1.055\times10^{-34}\ \text{J s}
$$

$$
L=4.22\times10^{-34}\ \text{J s}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{L=4\hbar}
$$

$$
\boxed{L=4.22\times10^{-34}\ \text{J s}}
$$
