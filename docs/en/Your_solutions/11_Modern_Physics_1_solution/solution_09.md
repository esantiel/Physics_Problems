# 9. Threshold Frequency

Work function:

$$
\phi=4.5\ \text{eV}
$$

Convert to joules:

$$
\phi=(4.5)(1.602\times10^{-19})
$$

$$
\phi=7.21\times10^{-19}\ \text{J}
$$

Threshold frequency:

$$
f_0=\frac{\phi}{h}
$$

$$
f_0=
\frac{7.21\times10^{-19}}
{6.626\times10^{-34}}
$$

$$
f_0\approx1.09\times10^{15}\ \text{Hz}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{f_0\approx1.1\times10^{15}\ \text{Hz}}
$$
