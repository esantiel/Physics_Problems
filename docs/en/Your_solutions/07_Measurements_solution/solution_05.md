## 5. Percentage Calculation

The measured time is:

$$
t = 5.45 \pm 0.22 \,\text{s}
$$

The percentage uncertainty is calculated using:

$$
\text{Percentage Uncertainty}
=
\frac{\Delta t}{t}\times 100\%
$$

Substituting the given values:

$$
\text{Percentage Uncertainty}
=
\frac{0.22}{5.45}\times 100\%
$$

$$
\text{Percentage Uncertainty}
=
0.04037 \times 100\%
$$

$$
\text{Percentage Uncertainty}
=
4.04\%
$$

Rounding appropriately:

$$
\text{Percentage Uncertainty} \approx 4.0\%
$$
