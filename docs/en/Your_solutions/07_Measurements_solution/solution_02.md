## 2. Propagation of Error II

The dimensions of the rectangular plate are:

$$
L = (15.3 \pm 0.1)\,\text{cm}
$$

$$
W = (8.4 \pm 0.1)\,\text{cm}
$$

The area is given by:

$$
A = LW
$$

Substituting the measured values:

$$
A = (15.3)(8.4)
$$

$$
A = 128.52 \,\text{cm}^2
$$

---

### Uncertainty Calculation

For multiplication, the relative uncertainties add:

$$
\frac{\Delta A}{A}
=
\frac{\Delta L}{L}
+
\frac{\Delta W}{W}
$$

Substituting the values:

$$
\frac{\Delta A}{A}
=
\frac{0.1}{15.3}
+
\frac{0.1}{8.4}
$$

$$
\frac{\Delta A}{A}
=
0.00654 + 0.01190
$$

$$
\frac{\Delta A}{A}
=
0.01844
$$

Now calculate the absolute uncertainty:

$$
\Delta A
=
A\left(\frac{\Delta A}{A}\right)
$$

$$
\Delta A
=
128.52(0.01844)
$$

$$
\Delta A
\approx 2.37 \,\text{cm}^2
$$

Rounding the uncertainty to two significant figures:

$$
\Delta A \approx 2.4 \,\text{cm}^2
$$

and rounding the area to the same decimal place:

$$
A \approx 128.5 \,\text{cm}^2
$$

---

## Final Answer

$$
A = (128.5 \pm 2.4)\,\text{cm}^2
$$
