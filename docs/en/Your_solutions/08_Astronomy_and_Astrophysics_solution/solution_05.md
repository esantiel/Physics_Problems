# 5. Escape Velocity

Escape velocity is

$$
v_e=\sqrt{\frac{2GM_M}{R_M}}
$$

Given:

$$
M_M=7.35\times10^{22}\,\text{kg}
$$

$$
R_M=1737\,\text{km}
=1.737\times10^6\,\text{m}
$$

Substituting:

$$
v_e\approx2375\,\text{m/s}
$$

$$
v_e\approx2.38\,\text{km/s}
$$

Relative to Earth:

$$
\frac{2.38}{11.2}
\approx0.212
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{v_e\approx2.38\,\text{km/s}}
$$

This is about

$$
\boxed{21.2\%}
$$

of Earth's escape velocity.
