# 8. Interplanetary Travel

Distance between Earth and Mars at closest approach:

$$
d = 55\times10^6\,\text{km}
$$

## a) Message Sent at the Speed of Light

Speed of light:

$$
c = 3.0\times10^5\,\text{km/s}
$$

Time:

$$
t = \frac{d}{c}
$$

$$
t = \frac{55\times10^6}{3\times10^5}
$$

$$
t \approx 183\,\text{s}
$$

$$
t \approx 3.05\,\text{minutes}
$$

### Answer

$$
\boxed{3.05\ \text{minutes}}
$$

---

## b) Spacecraft at 11.11 km/s

Speed:

$$
v = 11.11\,\text{km/s}
$$

Time:

$$
t = \frac{d}{v}
$$

$$
t = \frac{55\times10^6}{11.11}
$$

$$
t \approx 4.95\times10^6\,\text{s}
$$

Converting to days:

$$
t \approx 57.3\,\text{days}
$$

### Answer

$$
\boxed{57.3\ \text{days}}
$$

---

## c) Airplane at 900 km/h

Time:

$$
t = \frac{55\times10^6}{900}
$$

$$
t \approx 61111\,\text{hours}
$$

Converting to years:

$$
t \approx 6.97\,\text{years}
$$

### Answer

$$
\boxed{6.97\ \text{years}}
$$
