# 6. Solar Gravity

Using

$$
g=\frac{GM_S}{R_S^2}
$$

Given:

$$
M_S=2\times10^{30}\,\text{kg}
$$

$$
R_S=6.96\times10^8\,\text{m}
$$

Substituting:

$$
g\approx275\,\text{m/s}^2
$$

Relative to Earth:

$$
\frac{275}{9.81}
\approx28
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{g_{Sun}\approx275\,\text{m/s}^2}
$$

A person's weight would be approximately

$$
\boxed{28}
$$

times greater than on Earth.
