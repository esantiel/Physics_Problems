# 4. Geostationary Orbit

A geostationary satellite must have the same period as Earth:

$$
T=24\,\text{h}=86400\,\text{s}
$$

Using

$$
r=\left(\frac{GM_ET^2}{4\pi^2}\right)^{1/3}
$$

gives

$$
r\approx42164\,\text{km}
$$

The altitude above Earth's surface is

$$
h=r-R_E
$$

$$
h=42164-6378
$$

$$
h\approx35786\,\text{km}
$$

## Final Answer

Orbital period:

$$
\boxed{24\ \text{hours}}
$$

Altitude:

$$
\boxed{35786\ \text{km}}
$$
