# 7. Megastructures (Dyson Sphere)

Mass available:

$$
M = 3.3 \times 10^{23}\,\text{kg}
$$

Surface density:

$$
\sigma = 10\,\text{kg/m}^2
$$

The total area that can be constructed is

$$
A = \frac{M}{\sigma}
$$

Substituting:

$$
A = \frac{3.3\times10^{23}}{10}
$$

$$
A = 3.3\times10^{22}\,\text{m}^2
$$

For a sphere:

$$
A = 4\pi r^2
$$

Therefore,

$$
r = \sqrt{\frac{A}{4\pi}}
$$

Substituting:

$$
r = \sqrt{\frac{3.3\times10^{22}}{4\pi}}
$$

$$
r \approx 5.12\times10^{10}\,\text{m}
$$

Converting to kilometers:

$$
r \approx 5.12\times10^7\,\text{km}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{r \approx 5.1\times10^{10}\,\text{m}}
$$

or

$$
\boxed{r \approx 5.1\times10^7\,\text{km}}
$$
