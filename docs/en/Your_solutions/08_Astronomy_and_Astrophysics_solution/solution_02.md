# 2. Orbital Mechanics

ISS orbital radius:

$$
r = (6378+400)\,\text{km}
=6.778\times10^6\,\text{m}
$$

Using

$$
v=\sqrt{\frac{GM_E}{r}}
$$

with

$$
G=6.674\times10^{-11}
$$

$$
M_E=5.97\times10^{24}\,\text{kg}
$$

gives

$$
v_{ISS}\approx7.67\,\text{km/s}
$$

## Earth's Orbital Speed Around the Sun

Orbital circumference:

$$
C=2\pi(150\times10^6)
$$

$$
C\approx9.42\times10^8\,\text{km}
$$

Period:

$$
T=365.25\times24\times3600
$$

$$
T\approx3.156\times10^7\,\text{s}
$$

Thus

$$
v_E=\frac{C}{T}
\approx29.8\,\text{km/s}
$$

## Final Answer

ISS speed:

$$
\boxed{7.67\,\text{km/s}}
$$

Earth orbital speed:

$$
\boxed{29.8\,\text{km/s}}
$$

Earth moves about

$$
\boxed{3.9}
$$

times faster around the Sun than the ISS around Earth.
