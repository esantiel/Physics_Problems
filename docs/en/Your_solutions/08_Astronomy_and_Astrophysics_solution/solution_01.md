# 1. Rotational Velocity

Earth's radius:

$$
R_E = 6378\ \text{km}
$$

Earth rotates once every:

$$
T = 24 \times 3600 = 86400\ \text{s}
$$

The linear speed at the equator is

$$
v = \frac{2\pi R_E}{T}
$$

Substituting:

$$
v = \frac{2\pi(6378)}{86400}
$$

$$
v \approx 0.464\ \text{km/s}
$$

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{v \approx 0.465\ \text{km/s}}
$$
